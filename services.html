Services

Protect the Amazon, a branch of the Green Earth foundation is dedicated to protecting and revitalizing the amazon rainforest. The sad reality is that miles of this majestic forest burn down every day. Not only is this a tragic loss of beauty in the world, the amazon is one of the largest tools humanity has to fight against global warming. We would love you to join us on our journey of creating innovative solutions to protect one the the worlds most precious jewels. If you feel that this issue is important to you, please consider making a donation. We truly appreciate any amount of help that you can give.
