Services

Protect the amazon, a branch of the green foundation is dedicated to protecting and revitalizing the amazon rainforest. The sad reality is that miles of this majestic forest burn down every day. We are passisionate about creating innovative solutions to protect the rainforest. Not only is this a tragic loss of beauty in the world, the amazon is one of the largest tools humanity has to fight against global warming. We would love you to join us on our journey of protecting one the the worlds most precious jewels. If you feel that this issue is important and urgent please consider making a donation. We truly appreciate it and it will make real change.
